What is an Invoice Template?

An invoice template is a pre-designed format for billing your clients. It includes various necessary fields such as details of the seller and buyer, a description of the goods or services, the price, taxes, and total amount due. Our PowerPoint and Google Slides invoice templates not only save time but also ensure consistency, reducing the potential for errors and enhancing your business's professional image.

How Do I Issue a Legally Compliant Invoice?

Issuing a legally compliant invoice involves including specific information:

  • Seller's name and address
  • Buyer's name and address
  • Unique identification number
  • Date of the invoice
  • Description of the product or service
  • Quantity and price
  • Any applicable tax
  • Total amount due
Our editable invoice templates are designed with these legal requirements in mind. You simply fill in your details, and your professional, compliant invoice is ready.

What Fields Should be Included in an Invoice Template?

A comprehensive invoice should include the following fields:

  • Invoice number
  • Invoice date
  • Seller's business name and contact details
  • Buyer's business name and contact details
  • List of products/services with descriptions
  • Quantity of each product/service
  • Unit price of each product/service
  • Subtotal of costs
  • Any applicable sales tax
  • Total cost after tax
  • Payment terms and methods
  • Due date for payment
Our templates come equipped with these fields, ensuring you don't miss any critical invoice elements.
